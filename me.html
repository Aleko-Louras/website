About Me

Hi, I'm Aleko. I’m a passionate developer with a strong focus on web and mobile app development, working with React/React Native, Android Kotlin JPC, and iOS SwiftUI.

I thrive in front-end development—especially using reactive frameworks—because they enable simpler, more refined, and maintainable layouts through state management.

While back-end development isn’t my primary passion, I enjoy full-stack app creation, bringing together all the moving parts in applications we all use everyday.

I’m also interested and certified in cybersecurity. I focus on secure authentication and database management to ensure strong security in every project I build, as well as monitor for threats and partake in threat hunts.
